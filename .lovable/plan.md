
## DropFlow Landing Page

A modern, minimal landing page for DropFlow — a dropshipping automation platform connecting Shoper stores with AliExpress.

### Brand
- **Logo**: DropFlow water drop icon + wordmark (uploaded assets)
- **Colors**: Blue gradient theme inspired by the logo (#1E90FF → #00BFFF range), dark text, clean white/light gray backgrounds
- **Tone**: Professional, clear, efficiency-focused

### Structure

**1. Navigation Bar**
- DropFlow logo (icon + text) on the left
- "Go to App" button linking to app.dropflow.dev on the right

**2. Hero Section**
- Bold headline: "Automate Your Dropshipping Workflow"
- Subtitle explaining the Shoper → AliExpress automation value prop
- Primary CTA button → app.dropflow.dev
- Clean, minimal layout with generous whitespace

**3. Features Section (3 cards)**
- **Order Automation** — Auto-sync orders from Shoper to AliExpress, eliminate copy-paste errors
- **Profit Analytics** — Track revenue, margins, and product performance with real-time dashboards
- **Smart Export** — Export matched order data with calculated margins for accounting and analysis

**4. How It Works (3 steps, horizontal)**
- Connect your Shoper store
- Orders sync automatically to AliExpress
- Track everything from one dashboard

**5. Footer**
- DropFlow logo
- "Built by Kult Technology" with kulttechnology logo
- Link to app.dropflow.dev
- © 2025 DropFlow
